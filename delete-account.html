What happens when you delete your account

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All sighting reports you have submitted
  • All photos you have uploaded
  • Your username and profile information
  • All likes you have given

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

We will process your request within 7 days. You will receive a confirmation email when your data has been deleted.
